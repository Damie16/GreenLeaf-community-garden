Founders

GreenLeaf Community was founded in 2018 as a grassroots initiative to turn urban areas into a thriving community space. Founded by local residents Maria Santos and David Chen, the organisation began with just 12 garden plots and has to over 50 plots, a communal green house and educational spaces.

Mission and vision Statement

Mission statement: To create a network of thriving community gardens that empowers neighbourhoods to grow their own food and protect the environment. Vision statement: To cultivate sustainable food systems and community connections through urban gardening and education programs.

Community Impact

GreenLeaf Community Garden creates positive change by transforming unused urban spaces into thriving gardens that provide fresh food, education and a community. Through workshops, volunteer programs and sustainable gardening initiatives. the organisation empowers residents to connect with nature, support one another, and build sustainable community.
